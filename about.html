About Poli Secondary School

Poli Secondary School is a public school in Arumeru District dedicated to delivering quality education while nurturing each student’s potential.

Mission

To provide a safe and supportive learning environment where every student can grow academically, socially, and morally.

Vision

To become a leading secondary school in Tanzania through academic excellence, strong values, and active community participation.

Leadership

The school is led by a committed headteacher and a leadership team focused on student success, discipline, and continuous improvement.
